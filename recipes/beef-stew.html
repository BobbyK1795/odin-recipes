Beef Stew

Description

Beef stew is a hardy dish suitable for a cold winter day or just when you are in the mood for some tender meat, potatoes, and veggies. All the ingredients get to know each other in the broth, resulting in a stomach-filling bowl of stewy goodness.

Ingredients:

  • Beef, cut however you prefer (I like a chunkier stew)
  • Potatoes, diced
  • Carrots, sliced
  • Beef bouillon cubes.
  • Tomatoes, diced.
  • Corn (optional, some people don't like).

Recipe:

  1. Put all ingredients in a crock pot.
  2. Add a couple beef bouillon cubes.
  3. Fill crockpot half way with water.
  4. Let cook for 4 hours (longer for tenderer meat).
