Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken alfredo is a basic pasta dish, which is filling and delicious! At its core it is just a sauce, noodles, and chicken - it is easy to make and is sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • Penne Noodles (any kind of pasta will work and goes well with this dish, but I prefer penne noodles)
  • Milk (2% or whole milk works best)
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmessan cheese (shredded works best)
  • Seasonings of your choice (italian seasonings go well with this dish)
  • Chicken Breast (chopped or shredded depending on which you prefer)

Recipe:

  1. Make the sauce.
  2. Boil the pasta.
  3. Prepare the chicken.
  4. Add pasta and chicken to sauce.
  5. Add cheese, and seasonings to taste.
